About Us

The Deakin Data Science Club (DDSC) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of students passionate about data science, AI, and technology. We focus on three core areas to empower our members:

Employability

We connect members with industry professionals, host networking events, and provide career development opportunities to enhance employability.

Education

We organize workshops, tutorials, and study groups to help members develop technical skills and stay updated with the latest trends in data science.

Projects

We encourage members to collaborate on real-world projects, participate in hackathons, and showcase their work to build a strong portfolio.
